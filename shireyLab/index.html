We use butterflies and moths to better
understand human impacts on Earth's biodiversity.

Global Change Biology

We study the impacts of climate change, habitat loss, and more on butterfly and moth biodiversity.

Biodiversity Data Science

In doing so, we leverage tools from artificial intelligence and advanced modeling paradigms to make robust inferences.

Applied Conservation

We translate these inferences to applied conservation outcomes with NGO and policymaking partnerships.

We are based out of the McGuire Center for Lepidoptera & Biodiversity at the Florida Museum of Natural History - University of Florida starting in January 2025.
