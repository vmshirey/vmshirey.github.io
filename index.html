Vaughn Shirey

Hi there! I am a current PhD candidate at Georgetown University in Washington, DC working under the supervision of Dr. Leslie Ries in the Ries Lab of Butterfly Informatics. My current research focuses largely on butterfly responses to climate change in sub-Arctic and Arctic North America.

On this landing page you can find more information about my research, writing, and adventures in discovering new things about life on this planet.
