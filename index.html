Vaughn Shirey

Hi there! I am a current PhD candidate at Georgetown University in Washington, DC working under the supervision of Dr. Leslie Ries in the Ries Lab of Butterfly Informatics. My research focuses largely on butterfly responses to climate change in sub-Arctic and Arctic North America although I do dabble in a few side projects in other systems.

On this landing page you can find my digital CV. More information and links will be coming soon!
