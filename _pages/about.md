I am a PhD student in the Department of Biology at Georgetown University, located in Washington, DC. I work specifically in
the <a href="https://www.butterflyinformatics.org/" target="_blank">Ries Lab of Butterfly Informatics</a>, studying the evolutionary ecology of high-latitude and high-elevation butterflies in the
context of past, present, and future climate change. I am interested in projects that involve aggregating large amounts of data to conduct analyses and redistributing those data via robust informatics
infrastructure.
