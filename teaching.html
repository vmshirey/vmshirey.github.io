Graduate Teaching Experience

Global Climate Change Ecology (2020, 2021): This is an upper-level biology course for students interested in learning about the impacts of climate change on biodiversity. I organize and run a week long section of the course which introduces students to species distribution modeling. Students then explore machine learning approaches to constructing species distribution models on an organism of interest in lab. Lab in this course introduces students to R and the tidyverse package.

General Ecology (2019): This is an introductory level ecology class. I gave a guest lecture about global change impacts on biodiversity and assisted the professor in lab.
