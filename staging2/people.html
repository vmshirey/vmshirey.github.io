Principal Investigator




Dr. Vaughn M. Shirey

I am broadly interested in using natural history collections and monitoring data alongside emerging advances in data science to uncover the eco-evolutionary dynamics that underpin the responses of Lepidopteran communities and populations to global change.

I was a David H. Smith Postdoctoral Conservation Research Fellow at the University of Southern California and University of Florida. Before then, I obtained my Ph.D. at Georgetown University, was a Fulbright fellow at the Finnish Museum of Natural History, and obtained a B.S. in Environmental Science from Drexel University. I call the great city of Philadelphia my hometown.
