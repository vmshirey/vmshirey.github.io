Vaughn Shirey

Global Change Ecologist

I am a global change ecologist and biodiversity informatics specialist currently working towards a Ph.D. in ecology, evolution, and animal behavior at Georgetown University. My dissertation research focuses on the impacts of anthropogenic climate change on high-latitude butterfly communities and populations. I primarily use museum specimens and citizen science data to reconstruct historical ecologies.

I am currently looking for post-doctoral research opportunities beginning in Spring 2023.
