Life on our planet is rapidly changing...
I'm working on how we can detect that change

I am a biodiversity expert and data scientist working on how to leverage machine-learning to reconstruct historical ecologies and forecast how life on our planet is responding to global change. Along the way, I've developed an interest in cultivating community and in using art to communicate our shared ecological futures.
