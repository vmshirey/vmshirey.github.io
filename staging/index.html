Vaughn M. Shirey

About

Vaughn Shirey is an ecologist, data scientist, and Ph.D. candidate in the Ries Lab of Butterfly Informatics at Georgetown University. Their research interests include insect ecology (especially in highly seasonal environments), global change ecology, and the development/application of advanced models and machine learning tools for natural history museum data. Their work is currently supported by a National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellowship.

Originally from the Philadelphia area, Vaughn completed his undergraduate work at Drexel University in the Department of Biodiversity, Earth, and Environmental Sciences. Following thier undergraduate work, Vaughn was a Fulbright Finland Research Fellow at the Laboratory for Integrative Biodiversity Research at the Finnish Museum of Natural History in Helsinki.
