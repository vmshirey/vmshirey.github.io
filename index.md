Hi there and welcome to this landing page about me! I am a current PhD candidate in the Department of Biology at Georgetown University, working with Dr. Leslie Ries on detecting signals of butterfly biodiversity change in sparsely sampled regions, namely North American boreal forests and tundra.

My research interests include the use of digital biodiversity data, historical documentation, and hierarchical models for making inferences about ecological change over time, especially if tied to climate and land use. I work mostly with arthropods as indicator species to assess these threats to biodiversity.
